Features

Local network multiplayer

Compete against up to six friends in local network!

Google VR

The game is compatible with Google VR (formerly Google Cardboard) for an ultra-immersive experience!

Artificial Intelligence

Don't have friends to play with? No problem! Try to defeat AI-driven opponents!

Spectator mode

Tired of playing? You can also watch your friends while they play by joining the match as a spectator!

Tutorial

You don't know how to play? A tutorial will guide you through your first steps!

Made with unity

The game has been made with the amazing Unity Engine.
